SAMPLE: Updated Banking Policy (USA)
Version: 2.0 | Effective: Jan 2025
Issuing Authority: Internal Risk & Compliance

Section 1: Capital Requirements
Minimum Tier 1 capital ratio: 8.0% (INCREASED — mandatory compliance by Q2 2025)
Total capital ratio: 10.5% (INCREASED)
Leverage ratio: 4.0% (INCREASED)
Reporting cycle: Monthly (CHANGED from Quarterly)

Section 2: AML/KYC Procedures
Customer Due Diligence (CDD) required for all accounts.
Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) is now mandatory for all high-risk customers within 15 days.
Failure to complete EDD within deadline results in account suspension.
Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) must be filed within 15 calendar days (REDUCED from 30).
Penalty for late SAR filing: $5,000 per business day.

Section 3: Data Retention
Transaction records retained for 7 years (INCREASED from 5 years).
Customer identification documents retained for 7 years after account closure.
Digital records must be stored in immutable audit trail format per FinCEN guidance.

Section 4: Reporting Obligations
Monthly regulatory reports due within 20 days of month end (CHANGED).
Annual stress test results submitted to Federal Reserve by February 15 (CHANGED).
New: Climate risk disclosures required annually per Federal Reserve SR 22-6.

Section 5: Cybersecurity (NEW)
All institutions must implement multi-factor authentication.
Incident reporting to OCC required within 36 hours of material breach.
Annual penetration testing is mandatory.
